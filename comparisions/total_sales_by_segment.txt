OLDER FAMILIES / Budget — top segment by total sales ($154,012), and also has one of the largest customer counts (4,589). So its high revenue looks driven by having lots of customers, not necessarily big spenders individually.
YOUNG SINGLES/COUPLES / Mainstream — 2nd highest total sales ($143,832), but has by far the most customers of any segment (7,834 — nearly double Older Families/Budget). That's actually a red flag: with way more customers, you'd expect much higher total sales if each person spent similarly — so each individual in this segment might actually be spending less on average than a segment with fewer people but similar total sales.
RETIREES / Mainstream — 3rd highest sales ($141,969) with 6,318 customers — again a lot of customers.
